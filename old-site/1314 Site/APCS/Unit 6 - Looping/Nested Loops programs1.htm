		1 2 3 4 5 6

    		How many rows: 6

		1

  3. Write a program named Rectangles using nested loops to print the perimeter and area of rectangles using all combinations of lengths and widths running from 1 foot to 10 feet in increments of 1 foot. Print the output in headed columns. For example:

	1		1		4			1
	1		2		6			2
		. . .
