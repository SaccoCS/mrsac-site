Power Up

So far we have created three similar classes an added them to the Asteroids Game:  Asteroid, SpaceShip, and Bullet.  For each one of them, Mr. Sacco walked you through how to model the object and add it to the Canvas.  For this assignment, your job will be to design and implement a fourth item for the game, a PowerUp.

You will be in charge of creating the PowerUp class and incorporating it into your Asteroids Game.  Use the Asteroid, SpaceShip, and Bullet classes for inspiration.

A PowerUp is a single floating object that gives the spaceship a special ability when it comes in contact with it.

The PowerUp should start on the edge of the screen and drift in a random direction, similar to the Asteroids.

You choose how the PowerUp should paint itself.  A small circle, a floating letter P,a floating Sacco Face, etc...

When the ship comes in contact with the PowerUp, two things should happen:
1) A new PowerUp is constructed, causing it to appear on the edges again
2) The ship shoots bullets in all directions.  The number of bullets is up to you, but they should spread out evenly in all directions.asteroids
