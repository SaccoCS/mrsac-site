Score and Serve:

Now that the paddles are working, add a Scoring mechanism to make it feel more like a game.

Scoring:

Modify your game so that the ball no longer bounces off of the left or right wall. Instead you will put the ball in the center of the screen and increment the correct player's score. You can display the score using brush.drawString("Some String",x,y).

Random Serve:

After the point is scored, give the ball a random serve. You can accomplish this by picking a random xVector and yVector.
