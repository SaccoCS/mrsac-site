Adding Images:

 

Download the pack of Asteroid Images, which contains 5 Asteroids, a spaceship, a bullet, and a background.  Unzip and move all of the images into your project folder.

 

Now all we have to do is modify our painting methods to use the images instead of colored rectangles.

 

Reminders about images:


Instance field:

Image myImg;

 

Constructor:

myImg = Toolkit.getDefaultToolkit().getImage(getClass().getResource("SomeImage.png"));

 

When painting:

g.drawImage(myImg, x, y, width, height, null);
