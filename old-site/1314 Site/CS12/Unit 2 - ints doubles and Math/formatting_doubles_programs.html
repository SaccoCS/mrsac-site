Formatting doubles programs

Read over the Formatting Doubles notes and use them tho modify your programs in the following ways:

Averager - give only 3 digits after the decimal point.  Instead of 3.3333333333, it should give you 3.333

Circumference - give 4 digits after the decimal point

Taxer - This deals with money, so give 2 digits after the decimal place

WinLoss - Limit the percent to 3 digits after the decimal place
