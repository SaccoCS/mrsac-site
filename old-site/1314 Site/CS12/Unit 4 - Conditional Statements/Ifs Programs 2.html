Ifs Programs 2:  AND and OR

Complete each of the following program using either an AND or an OR
&&   ||

 

1)  TargetAudience:  Many companies direct their advertisement to people of the Ages 18 to 39 because they are amongst the biggest spenders.  Write a program named TargetAudience, which will ask the user for their age and tell them if they are the target audience or not.

Please enter your age:
25
You are important

Please enter your age:
42
Nobody cares about you

Please enter your age:
9
Nobody cares about you

2)  LuckyNumber:  Mr. Sacco considers a number to be lucky if it is either greater than 100 or if it's divisible by 5 ( when modded by 5 the remainder is 0).  Write a program named LuckyNumber which asks the user for a number and then states if it's lucky or not.

Please enter a number:
15
15 is a lucky number

Please enter a number:
27
27 is an unlucky number

Please enter a number:
101
101 is a lucky number

 

Hint for the next two:  You may chain multiple &&'s and ||'s together in one statement.  For example:  Checking if a number either 2, 6, or 9 would look like if( num == 2 || num == 6 || num == 9)
